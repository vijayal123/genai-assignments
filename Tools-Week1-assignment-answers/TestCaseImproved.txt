Improved Test Case  
**Test Case ID:** TC_UPI_001  
**Title:** Successful P2P Fund Transfer via UPI  
**Pre‑conditions:**  
- User is logged into the UPI app (GPay/PhonePe/BHIM).  
- User has a valid UPI ID registered with the bank.  
- User’s bank account has a balance ≥ ₹200.  
- Device is running Android 13 (or iOS 17) with the latest app version.  
- Test data file “UPI_TestData.xlsx” contains:  
  - Recipient UPI ID: receiver@bank  
  - Transfer amount: ₹100  
  - UPI PIN: 123456  

**Execution Steps:**  
1. Launch the UPI application.  
2. Tap the “Send Money” or “Pay” button.  
3. In the “Recipient” field, enter the UPI ID from the test data.  
4. Verify that the UPI ID is validated and the “Next” button is enabled.  
5. Enter the transfer amount from the test data.  
6. Verify that the amount is displayed correctly in the confirmation screen.  
7. Tap “Pay”.  
8. When prompted, enter the 6‑digit UPI PIN from the test data.  
9. Confirm the transaction.  

**Expected Results:**  
- A “Transaction Successful” screen appears with the exact text “Transaction Successful”.  
- A debit notification is received on the sender’s device: “₹100 has been debited from your account”.  
- A credit notification is received on the receiver’s device: “₹100 has been credited to your account”.  
- The sender’s bank balance decreases by ₹100 (verified via the app’s balance screen).  
- The transaction appears in the “Transaction History” list with a unique UTR number and the correct amount, date, and time.  

**Post‑conditions:**  
- The UPI transaction is recorded in the bank’s transaction log.  
- No residual test data remains on the device.
